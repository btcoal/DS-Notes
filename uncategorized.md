# Uncategorized

* Self-supervised learning – SimCLR, Bootstrap Your Own Latent (BYOL), Masked AutoEncoders (MAE) (see https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2021-05-31-contrastive/)

* Domain adaptation – fine-tuning vs freezing vs adapters.

* Few-shot / zero-shot learning – prompt-based adaptation.

* Hyperparameter search – Bayesian optimization, Hyperband, Optuna.

* Dataset versioning & reproducibility – DVC, W&B Artifacts.

* Data leakage – temporal, target, or leakage through feature engineering.

* Video generation/editing:

    * Maintaining temporal coherence.

    * Conditioning on reference frames or scripts.
